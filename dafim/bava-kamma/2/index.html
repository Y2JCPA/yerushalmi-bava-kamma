תלמוד ירושלמי בבא קמא

🐂 Daf ב

The Yerushalmi opens Nezikin by defining the core categories of liability.

Source: Jerusalem Talmud Bava Kamma 1:1 · Sefaria Yerushalmi is structured by chapter/halakhah/segment. This prototype anchors daf ב to the opening halakhah where the Venice Columns overlay marks 2a.

The whole sugya in one sentence

The Mishnah lists four primary damage categories, then the Yerushalmi begins identifying each one from the pesukim.

The point is not just naming cases. It is building a framework for when a person becomes responsible for damage caused by his property or actions.

ארבעה אבות נזיקין

🐂

השור

The ox, understood here as keren: goring damage.

🕳️

הבור

The pit, damage caused by a hazard someone created.

🐾

המבעה

A category the Bavli debates. The Yerushalmi’s framing needs careful tracking.

🔥

ההבער

Fire, damage that spreads from a person’s force or property.

How the Mishnah reasons

1

Each av has a distinct feature.

2

No single category can teach all the others.

3

The common denominator: דרכן להזיק, their normal way is to cause damage.

4

Because שמירתן עליך, guarding them is your responsibility.

5

When they damage, the mazikin pays from מיטב הארץ.

Source anchor

אַרְבָּעָה אָבוֹת נְזִיקִין: הַשּׁוֹר וְהַבּוֹר וְהַמַּבְעֶה וְהַהֶבְעֵר... הַצַּד הַשָּׁוֶה שֶׁבָּהֶן שֶׁדַּרְכָּן לְהַזִּיק וּשְׁמִירָתָן עָלֶיךָ...
There are four main categories of damages: the bull, the pit, the maveh, and the fire... Their common denominator is that their way is to cause damage and their guarding is upon you.

Yerushalmi-specific note

The first technical issue for this project is pagination. The Yerushalmi API does not behave like Bavli daf/amud refs. The build pipeline should map Sefaria chapter/halakhah segments to the printed Yerushalmi page markers before generating all pages.

Quick review

1. What are the four avot listed in the opening Mishnah?

The Mishnah’s list is השור, הבור, המבעה, וההבער.

2. What is the shared principle tying the avot together?

The Mishnah says דרכן להזיק ושמירתן עליך.

3. What is the prototype’s main technical caution?

Refs such as 1:1 are reliable; daf/page mapping needs to use overlay markers.
