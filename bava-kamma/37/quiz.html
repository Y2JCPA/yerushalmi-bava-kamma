Yerushalmi Bava Kamma 37 Quiz

5 questions · Self-graded · Test your learning!
QUESTION 1 OF 5
What does the Mishnah hold about 'סמא את עיני על מנת לפטור'?
A. He is exempt — consent works
B. He is liable — bodily damage cannot be waived
C. He pays only half
D. Only the victim's family can sue
Correct: B — Bodily damage cannot be waived: 'על מנת לפטור חייב'. Property damage, however, can be waived.
QUESTION 2 OF 5
How does R' Lazar read the Mishnah's 'on condition not to be liable'?
A. A 'yes' that means 'yes' — sincere consent
B. A 'yes' that really means 'no' (בהן שהוא כלאו)
C. A formal court declaration
D. Only with witnesses
Correct: B — R' Lazar: the Mishnah is dealing with a 'yes' which really means 'no'. Reish Laqish takes the opposite stance.
QUESTION 3 OF 5
If Gentiles demand 'X's money specifically' and the Jew hands it over, what is his status?
A. Liable — he handed it over
B. Liable for half
C. Exempt — even if he handed it over (R' Yose)
D. It depends on witnesses
Correct: C — R' Yose: 'אבל ממון פלוני אפילו נטל ונתן ביד פטור.' Specificity by the coercer breaks the chain of responsibility.
QUESTION 4 OF 5
What does 'משלם כשעת הגזילה' mean in the opening Mishnah of Chapter 9?
A. He pays the current market price
B. He pays the value at the time of the robbery
C. He pays double
D. He pays whatever the victim demands
Correct: B — The general principle: 'זה הכלל כל הגזלנין משלמין כשעת הגזילה.' Once שינוי occurs, the price is locked at the moment of theft.
QUESTION 5 OF 5
What is R' Yudan's framing of the bleaching/שינוי dispute?
A. Bleaching is always a שינוי
B. Bleaching is never a שינוי
C. It is a leniency for the gazlan that he pays כשעת הגזילה
D. It depends on the type of wool
Correct: C — R' Yudan: 'קל הוא שהיקלו בגזלן שישלם כשעת הגזילה.' The whole framework is a calibrated leniency for the robber.
