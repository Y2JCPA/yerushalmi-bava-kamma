Yerushalmi Bava Kamma 43 Quiz

5 questions · Self-graded · Test your learning!
QUESTION 1 OF 5
Why may one use mistakenly switched vessels at the artisan but not at a wedding?
A. The artisan owns them anyway
B. The artisan implicitly authorizes use of the switched item; a wedding/mourner's house does not
C. Weddings are exempt from monetary rules
D. Artisans are presumed to be thieves
Correct: B — R' Hiyya's example of Bar Qora: every taking and depositing in such a shop is בִּרְשׁוּת.
QUESTION 2 OF 5
What legal mechanism transfers ownership when publicans seize a donkey?
A. Kinyan chatzer
B. Yi'ush — owner's despair
C. Public sale
D. Court decree
Correct: B — מִפְּנֵי שֶׁהַבְּעָלִין מִתְיָיאֲשִׁין מֵהֶן — the owners give up hope.
QUESTION 3 OF 5
According to R' Yishmael b. R' Yochanan b. Beroka, on what basis may one cut a branch to retrieve a swarm?
A. Rabbinic decree of the Sanhedrin
B. A court stipulation rooted in Joshua's distribution of the Land
C. An explicit Torah verse
D. Local custom in the Galilee
Correct: B — שֶׁעַל מְנָת כֵּן הִנְחִיל יְהוֹשֻׁעַ לְיִשְׂרָאֵל אֶת הָאָרֶץ.
QUESTION 4 OF 5
Why does the Mishnah require the holder of recognized stolen books to swear at all (per R' Abba bar Mamal)?
A. Because oaths always accompany returns
B. So that householders will not become fences for thieves
C. Because books are especially holy
D. To embarrass the buyer
Correct: B — שֶׁלֹּא יְהוּ בַעֲלֵי בָתִּים נִטְפָּלִין לַגַּנָּבִים.
QUESTION 5 OF 5
If a man's donkey is being swept away and he cries 'I did not despair!', what does R' Yochanan rule?
A. He is automatically believed
B. חֲזָקָה מְיָיאֵשׁ הוּא — presumption stands that he despaired
C. Reish Lakish's view that crying proves no yi'ush is followed
D. The donkey returns automatically
Correct: B — R' Yochanan holds the presumption of yi'ush stands even against the cry; Reish Lakish disagrees.
