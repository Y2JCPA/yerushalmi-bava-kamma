Yerushalmi Bava Kamma 31 Quiz

5 questions · Self-graded · Test your learning!
QUESTION 1 OF 5
Which of the following does the Mishnah say still incurs ד׳ וה׳?
A. Stealing and slaughtering on Shabbat
B. Stealing land
C. Stealing a document
D. Stealing from a thief
Correct: A — גָּנַב וְטָבַח בְּיוֹם הַכִּיפּוּרִים, on Shabbat, or for ע״ז — all still trigger ד׳ וה׳ per the Mishnah.
QUESTION 2 OF 5
What two cases does R' Shimon exempt from ד׳ וה׳?
A. Stealing on Shabbat and stealing on Yom Kippur
B. Slaughter for medicine/dogs and slaughter that turned out טריפה
C. Stealing from one's father and dedicating to the Temple
D. Theft of an ox and theft of a sheep
Correct: B — רִבִּי שִׁמְעוֹן פּוֹטֵר בִּשְׁנֵי אֵילּוּ — לרפואה/לכלבים, and שוחט ונמצא טריפה.
QUESTION 3 OF 5
What principle does R' Bun bar Chiyya derive from כְּדֵי רִשְׁעָתוֹ?
A. The court only imposes one of two penalties when both are court-penalties
B. All sinners are punished twice
C. Only the wicked are liable for ד׳ וה׳
D. Penalties are halved on Yom Kippur
Correct: A — שְׁנֵי דְבָרִים מְסוּרִין לְבֵית דִּין הַתּוֹפֵס אֶחָד מֵהֶן. A court-penalty + a heavenly penalty can stack; two court-penalties cannot.
QUESTION 4 OF 5
If only the second pair (slaughter/sale witnesses) is shown to be perjured, what do they pay?
A. כפל
B. תשלומי ארבעה
C. תשלומי שלושה — the increment from 2× to 5×
D. Nothing
Correct: C — He still pays כפל for the theft; the second pair pays the תשלומי שלושה they tried falsely to impose.
QUESTION 5 OF 5
Why does perjury in the FIRST pair (theft witnesses) collapse the entire case?
A. Because the first pair are senior witnesses
B. שֶׁאִם אֵין גְּנֵיבָה אֵין טְבִיחָה וְאֵין מְכִירָה — without theft there is nothing to slaughter or sell
C. Because two pairs cannot testify together
D. Because of כְּדֵי רִשְׁעָתוֹ
Correct: B — Theft is the foundation. Knock that out and slaughter/sale lose their object — both testimonies are nullified.
