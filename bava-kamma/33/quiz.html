Yerushalmi Bava Kamma 33 Quiz

5 questions · Self-graded · Test your learning!
QUESTION 1 OF 5
What does R' Lazar say about someone seen slaughtering a black ox in the forest?
A. He is exempt because the forest is הפקר
B. חֲזָקָה גָּנוּב הוּא — there is a presumption it was stolen
C. He must bring two witnesses
D. He pays only כפל
Correct: B — R' Lazar establishes a chazakah of theft, and the Yerushalmi clarifies that this applies specifically when he was seen dragging it — בְּשֶׁרָאוּהוּ מוֹשֵׁךְ.
QUESTION 2 OF 5
What unique liability does a slaughterer have that a seller does not?
A. He pays double
B. He is criminally liable on Shabbat (slaughter is a melachah)
C. He cannot be a שליח
D. He must reimburse the מוכר
Correct: B — הַטּוֹבֵחַ בַּשַׁבָּת חַיָיב וְהַמּוֹכֵר בַּשַׁבָּת פָּטוּר. R' Yose b. R' Bun explains the apparent counter-case: it's the tearing from the ground, not the sale itself, that creates Shabbat liability.
QUESTION 3 OF 5
According to Rebbi, when does selling 'except for X' still trigger ד׳ וה׳?
A. Only if X is the horn
B. Only if X is the wool
C. When X is a vital organ — דָּבָר שֶׁהַנְשָׁמָה תְלוּיָה בּוֹ
D. Only when X is sold to a third party
Correct: C — Rebbi's test: if the retained part is a vital organ, the buyer's purchase is meaningful and ד׳ וה׳ applies; if not (e.g. wool, horn), the seller is exempt.
QUESTION 4 OF 5
If a thief was drawing the animal out but it died while still on the owner's property, what is the law?
A. Liable for ד׳ וה׳
B. פָּטוּר — the kinyan of theft is not yet complete
C. Liable for double only
D. Liable only if there were witnesses
Correct: B — The Mishnah teaches that drawing alone, while still on the owner's premises, does not complete the theft. The thief is liable only after הגבהה or once it leaves the property — and R' Mana even asks whether the exemption extends to basic theft as well.
QUESTION 5 OF 5
Which is NOT one of the Mishnah's restrictions on raising animals?
A. אֵין מְגַדְּלִין בְּהֵמָה דַקָּה בְּאֶרֶץ יִשְׂרָאֵל
B. אֵין מְגַדְּלִין תַּרְנְגוֹלִין בִּירוּשָׁלַיִם
C. לֹא יְגַדֵּל אָדָם אֶת הַכֶּלֶב אֶלָּא אִם כֵּן הָיָה קָשׁוּר בַּשַׁלְשֶׁלֶת
D. אֵין מְגַדְּלִין סוּסִים בְּסוּרְיָא
Correct: D — The Mishnah bans small livestock in EY (permitted in Syria and the מדברות), chickens in Yerushalayim and for kohanim anywhere, pigs everywhere for a Jew, and unchained dogs. It says nothing about horses in Syria.
