📖 Overview — Where רגל Is Full and Where It Is Only Half

וּמַה פְשִׁיטָא לוֹן. בְּנֶזֶק שָׁלֵם. וּמַה צְרִיכָה לוֹן. בַּחֲצִי נֶזֶק
The sugya: The daf opens mid-conversation between R' Hoshaia the Elder and R' Yudan Nesia. R' Abba bar Mamal walks in and asks about a mule that wags its tail and breaks something — is the owner liable in full, or only half? The daf then runs through a series of cases where one act of regel-style movement causes layered damage: a skin of oil, glass on a table, two stacked tables, and a ricochet from public into private property. The cases all probe one question: when normal walking damage breaks something the animal does not normally break, where does חֲצִי נֶזֶק begin?

💬 The Mule That Wagged Its Tail

R' Abba bar Mamal walks into the beit midrash with a question.
R' Abba
כִּישְׁכְּשָׁה בִּזְנָבָהּ. כְּגוֹן אִילֵּין פֵּירְדָתָא. מָהוּ? If a mule wags its tail and breaks something — what is the law?
Silence
וְלָא אָמְרִין לֵיהּ כְּלוּם — at first they said nothing.
R' Hoshaia
If not, what would we say? צָרִיךְ שֶׁיְּהֵא תוֹפֵשׂ בִּזְנָבָהּ — must the owner be holding its tail? Only when the tail-wagging is unusual. If it is normal — yes, the owner must literally hold the tail.
Y
Bottom line: פְשִׁיטָא לוֹן בְּנֶזֶק שָׁלֵם — full damages were obvious. צְרִיכָה לוֹן בַּחֲצִי נֶזֶק — what they were unsure about was the half-damage case.
Key move: The Yerushalmi distinguishes between the animal's regular behavior (full damage, normal regel) and its unusual behavior (half damage, like keren). The mule's wagging tail forces a fresh ruling at the seam between the two.

📊 Layered Damage Cases (R' Yirmiyah's Series)

When one stomp breaks two things, each layer is judged on its own.
CaseOuter / Direct ObjectInner / Indirect Object
Skin (נוֹד) full of oilנֶזֶק שָׁלֵם for the נוֹדחֲצִי נֶזֶק for the שֶׁמֶן
Table with glass on topנֶזֶק שָׁלֵם for the טַבֶּלָּהחֲצִי נֶזֶק for the זְכוּכִית
Two tables stackedנֶזֶק שָׁלֵם for the upperחֲצִי נֶזֶק for the lower
Pattern: What the foot directly tramples is normal regel — full damage. What gets broken indirectly, by the trampled object collapsing, is treated like keren-style abnormal damage — only half.

🏃 The Ricochet Case — רה"ר → רה"י

R' Yirmiyah's hardest case: where does liability attach?
טַבֶּלָּה מוּנַחַת בִּרְשׁוּת הָרַבִּים
Animal steps on it in the public domain
↓
הִתִּיזָה וְהָלְכָה וְנִשְׁתַּבְּרָה בִרְשׁוּת הַיָּחִיד
The piece flies off and shatters in private property
↓
Follow the stepping
אַחַר דְּרִיסָתָהּ — פָּטוּר
Stepping was in רה"ר → exempt
Follow the breaking
אַחַר שְׁבִירָתָהּ — חַיָיב
Breaking was in רה"י → liable
↓
The Yerushalmi leaves the question framed as a clean fork: do we trace liability to the act of pressure or to the moment of breakage?
Why this matters: Domain (רה"ר vs. רה"י) is decisive for רגל liability. The ricochet case forces us to identify exactly when the damage 'happened' — a question that returns later in the masechet.

🐔 Entering Chapter 2:2 — The Mishnah of Chickens

The new Mishnah at the end of the daf sets up the next sugya.

🐔 Normal walking

  • הַתַּרְנוֹגַלִּין מוּעָדִין לְהַלֵּךְ כְּדַרְכָּן וּלְשַׁבֵּר
  • Chickens are 'notorious' for breaking things just by walking
  • Implies full damage for ordinary stride breakage

🪢 Tied thread on foot

  • הָיָה דְלִיל קָשׁוּר בְּרַגְלוֹ
  • A string trailing from the foot snags vessels
  • Pays half damage

🪶 Scraping / hopping

  • שֶׁהָיָה מְהַדֵּס וְשִׁיבֵּר
  • Hopping/scraping motion that knocks vessels
  • Pays half damage
Setup for daf 9: The Mishnah seeds the next debate: when is a tied thread the owner's fault (he tied it) versus an act-of-nature (it tied itself)? Daf 9 opens with Rav Huna's distinction.

🧠 The Underlying Principle

How the Yerushalmi reads the chain of cases on this daf.
1️⃣ Identify whether the damage is the animal's normal way (דַּרְכָּהּ) or unusual (אֵין דַּרְכָּהּ).
↓
2️⃣ Normal way → רגל or שן territory → נֶזֶק שָׁלֵם in the victim's domain.
↓
3️⃣ Unusual / indirect → keren-style damage → חֲצִי נֶזֶק.
↓
4️⃣ When one act produces both — like a stomp + a glass shatter — split the payment per layer.
↓
5️⃣ Domain matters: even full-damage cases can collapse if the act started in רה"ר.
Bottom line: The daf's whole engine is the seam between regel and keren. Every case asks: which side of that line are we on, and where in space and time did the damage occur?
