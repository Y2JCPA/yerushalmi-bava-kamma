Yerushalmi Bava Kamma 22 Quiz

5 questions · Self-graded · Test your learning!
QUESTION 1 OF 5
What does 'ובעל השור נקי' exclude according to one tannaitic reading?
A. Payment for damages
B. Payment for a slave
C. Payment for kofer
D. Payment for a wild animal
Correct: B — One reading derives 'נקי מדמי עבד' — exempt from paying for a slave even though slave damages normally apply.
QUESTION 2 OF 5
What does Reish Lakish say R' Hoshaia explained about 'shor mit'chakhech bakotel'?
A. It's about a tame bull only
B. It teaches kofer after death
C. It only applies in a public domain
D. The owner pays nezek
Correct: B — Reish Lakish: R' Hoshaia, the father of the Mishnah, taught that this Mishnah is about the kofer payment after death.
QUESTION 3 OF 5
According to R' Yehudah, why are the hekdesh and ger's bulls exempt from death?
A. Because the verse only mentions tame bulls
B. Because they have no owners — אין להם בעלים
C. Because they are not in a settled area
D. Because the witnesses are invalid
Correct: B — R' Yehudah derives from 'והועד בבעליו' that an animal needs an owner to be subject to the death penalty.
QUESTION 4 OF 5
What does Rav say about a sold bull that turns out to be a gorer?
A. Sale is valid as is
B. It is מקח טעות — a mistaken sale
C. Buyer pays half
D. Seller becomes liable for damages
Correct: B — Rav: it's a mistaken sale; Shmuel: the seller can claim he sold it for slaughter.
QUESTION 5 OF 5
What is the key dispute between R' Yochanan and Reish Lakish about a bull going to be stoned with zomemim witnesses?
A. Whether the bull can be slaughtered
B. Whether 'first to grab acquires' or it's יאוש טעות
C. Whether the owner pays kofer
D. Whether the witnesses pay
Correct: B — R' Yochanan: first to grab acquires; Reish Lakish: it's despair based on a mistake and doesn't transfer ownership.
