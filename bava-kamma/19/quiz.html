5 questions · Self-graded · Test your learning!
QUESTION 1 OF 5
Why does the Yerushalmi distinguish stolen סלעים גסות from stolen אבנים דקות?
A. Because coins are mu'ad and stones are tam
B. Because large coins can be subdivided exactly while small stones cannot, so doubt requires each partner to take by his purse
C. Because stones are forbidden as collateral
D. Because R' Eleazar disqualifies stones
Correct: B — R' Shammai: סלעים are large enough to subdivide exactly. With small stones, לֵית יְדַע אִי מִן הָדֵין נְסַב, so each takes proportional to his investment.
QUESTION 2 OF 5
What does R' Yehudah rule about a bull mu'ad on Shabbatot but not weekdays?
A. Always pays full damage
B. Always pays half damage
C. Pays full damage on Shabbat, half on weekdays — and reverts to תם only after three Shabbat days of changed behavior
D. Such a category does not exist
Correct: C — בַּשַּׁבָּתוֹת מְשַׁלֵּם נֶזֶק שָׁלֵם וּבִימוֹת הַחוֹל מְשַׁלֵּם חֲצִי נֶזֶק, and reverting requires שלשת ימי שבתות.
QUESTION 3 OF 5
What is the Yerushalmi's tentative reason for R' Yehudah's Shabbat-only rule?
A. Shabbat law overrides nezikin
B. מִכֵּיוָן דּוּ חֲמִי לוֹן לְבִישִׁין נְקִיִין עוֹד הוּא מְשַׁנֶּה דַּעְתֵּיהּ — seeing people in clean clothes, the ox changes its behavior
C. Sabbaths have shorter days
D. The owner is more careful on Shabbat
Correct: B — The Yerushalmi suggests the ox is reacting to the visual cue of clean Shabbat clothing.
QUESTION 4 OF 5
What does Rav Huna report about the niddah-with-a-gap question?
A. Rav definitely said וודאי
B. Rav definitely said ספק
C. Rav first said וודאי, reversed himself, and finally said ספק; Rav Ada only heard the first answer
D. It was never decided
Correct: C — Rav Huna says he was at the entire conversation: ברישא, באמצעיתא, ובסיפא, and Rav landed on ספק.
QUESTION 5 OF 5
Why did Rabban Gamliel decree that robbing a non-Jew is forbidden, given the Mishnah's exemption rule?
A. He rejected the Mishnah
B. Because of חִילּוּל הָשֵׁם after the two Roman officials objected
C. Because of a kal va-chomer from שור הקדש
D. Because the Romans paid him to change it
Correct: B — בְּאוֹתָהּ שָׁעָה גָּזַר רַבָּן גַּמְלִיאֵל עַל גְּזֵילוֹת נָכְרִי שֶׁיְּהֵא אָסוּר מִפְּנֵי חִילּוּל הָשֵׁם. The rule was tightened so Torah would not be desecrated.
