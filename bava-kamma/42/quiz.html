Yerushalmi Bava Kamma 42 Quiz

5 questions · Self-graded · Test your learning!
QUESTION 1 OF 5
What is the dispute between R' Ḥizqiah and R' Yose about the mishmar Mishnah?
A. Whether the case is across two weeks or within one week
B. Whether the asham is valid
C. Whether kohanim can switch shifts
D. Whether the chomesh is paid first
Correct: A — בִּשְׁתֵּי שַׁבָּתוֹת פְּלִיגִין vs. בְּשַׁבָּת אַחַת. Each reading produces a different halakhic implication.
QUESTION 2 OF 5
According to the Yerushalmi, when can a sacrifice be removed from the hands of a priestly watch?
A. When it was received outside their week, or when they were lazy and didn't offer it in time
B. Only when the kohen sins
C. Only on Shabbat
D. Never — once received it always stays
Correct: A — שֶׁזָּכוּ בַּמִּשְׁמָר שֶׁלֹּא בְשַׁבָּתָן or נִתְעַצְּלוּ ולֹא הִקְרִיבוּהוּ — מוֹצִיאִין אוֹתָן מִיָּדוֹ.
QUESTION 3 OF 5
When are heirs of a robber required to pay back stolen property?
A. When their father left them mortgageable property — דָבָר שֶׁיֵּשׁ בּוֹ אַחֵרָיוּת
B. Never — heirs are always exempt
C. Only if the gezeilah is identifiable
D. Always — heirs are fully liable
Correct: A — The Mishnah: אִם הָיָה דָבָר שֶׁיֵּשׁ בּוֹ אַחֵרָיוּת נְכָסִים חַיָיבִין לְשַׁלֵּם.
QUESTION 4 OF 5
What does Reish Lakish say about 'דבר שיש בו אחריות' in this Mishnah?
A. It means the actual stolen item — גוף הגזילה — is in the heirs' hands
B. It means real estate only
C. It means a written contract
D. It means money in a chest
Correct: A — בְשֶׁהִנִּיחַ לִפְנֵיהֶן גּוּף הַגְּזֵילָה. R' Yonatan reads קרקע; Reish Lakish reads the physical stolen item.
QUESTION 5 OF 5
If A robs a tallit and gives it to B, can the original owner reclaim it from B?
A. Yes — מוֹצִיאִין אַף מִן הַשֵּׁינִי
B. Never — only from A
C. Only if B paid for it
D. Only if A is dead
Correct: A — R' Yoḥanan in the name of R' Yannai, and R' Abba bar Mamal even attributes this to R' Ḥiyya: the tallit can be recovered even from the second holder.
